About SoulNotes

Created with love by Morola Omotoso 💖

SoulNotes is a Christian mood and scripture journal that helps you reflect, release, and renew your spirit. Select your mood, receive Bible verses and encouragement, and track your emotional journey—all in a peaceful, pastel-colored space. This project was built by a 15-year-old passionate about coding and Christ. 🙏
